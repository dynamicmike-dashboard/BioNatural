# BioNatural Phase 2.3: Video Production Roadmap (Reels & TikTok)

This roadmap outlines the transition from static luxury imagery to **Short-Form Video Synthesis**.

## 🎥 The Concept: "Wellness in Motion"
To compete in the Playa del Carmen and international wellness markets, we are implementing an AI Video Pipeline that focuses on **Sensory Experience** and **Bilingual Education**.

### 1. Pure Essence: Rosemary & Biotin Shampoo
*   **Visual Narrative:** A "Nature-to-Bottle" slow-motion sequence. Starts with high-fidelity water droplets on fresh rosemary sprigs, transitioning to the rich, botanical lathering process in soft morning light.
*   **Objective:** Demonstrate hair strength and volume.
*   **Audio:** Ambient nature sounds + 15s bilingual voiceover.

### 2. Pure Essence: Eye Contour Gel
*   **Visual Narrative:** A "Cooling Refresh" routine. Macro shot of the clear gel texture being applied to cooling jade rollers, followed by a soft-focus lifestyle shot of a woman looking refreshed against a Tulum-style interior.
*   **Objective:** Focus on de-puffing and restorative morning routines.
*   **Audio:** Lo-fi chill wellness track.

### 3. Supplement Series: The "Daily Green" (Chlorophyll)
*   **Visual Narrative:** Pure liquid chlorophyll dispersing in slow motion into a glass of artisanal water. The visual highlights the "Oxygenation" and "Cellular Detox" through the deep emerald green pigmentation.
*   **Objective:** Energy and detox education.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
*   **Synthesis Engine:** Optimized for **VEO3 / Sora** (High Fidelity).
*   **Frame Rate:** Constant 60fps for luxury smoothness.
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (Mobile Portrait).
*   **Production Rule:** Batch rendering follows the final approval of the 30-day caption calendar to ensure maximum coherence.
